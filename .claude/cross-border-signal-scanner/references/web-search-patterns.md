# Web Search Patterns

Optimized search queries for each signal type. Run 2-3 queries per type for daily scans. For targeted scans, prepend the company name to each query.

---

## 1. Mergers & Acquisitions

```
"cross-border acquisition" announced 2026
"international merger" completed OR closing 2026
"acquired" "based in" (Japan OR Germany OR France OR China OR Korea OR Brazil)
company acquisition "regulatory approval" multilingual OR translation
```

**Targeted variant**: `"{company_name}" acquisition OR merger international`

---

## 2. International Expansion

```
"opening office" (Europe OR Asia OR "Latin America") 2026
"expanding into" new market international 2026
"international expansion" announced hiring 2026
"entering the" market (Japan OR Germany OR France OR Brazil OR India) 2026
```

**Targeted variant**: `"{company_name}" expansion OR "new office" OR "entering" international`

---

## 3. Regulatory Compliance

```
"multilingual documentation" required compliance 2026
"regulatory compliance" "multiple languages" OR localization
EU MDR compliance documentation translation
"financial regulation" compliance multilingual reporting
```

**Targeted variant**: `"{company_name}" compliance OR regulation multilingual OR translation`

---

## 4. Cross-Border Partnerships

```
"joint venture" international announced 2026
"distribution agreement" (Europe OR Asia OR "Latin America") 2026
"strategic partnership" cross-border 2026
"licensing agreement" international market 2026
```

**Targeted variant**: `"{company_name}" partnership OR "joint venture" international`

---

## 5. New Market Entry

```
"product launch" (Europe OR Asia OR "Latin America") 2026
"localization manager" OR "translation lead" job posting
"entering" new market localization 2026
"expanding" (Japan OR Germany OR France OR Brazil) launch 2026
```

**Targeted variant**: `"{company_name}" launch OR localization OR "new market"`

---

## Query Tips

- Use date filters where available (past 24 hours for daily, past week for catch-up)
- Prefer news sources over forum posts for signal quality
- Cross-reference: if a company appears in 2+ signal types, it's a stronger lead
- Skip results from translation/localization companies themselves -- they're competitors, not prospects
- Skip results about Bluente or direct competitors
