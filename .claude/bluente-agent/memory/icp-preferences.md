## Starting Criteria (from call transcript 2026-03-13)

### Target companies
- 50-5000 employees (sweet spot for translation volume without existing TMS)
- Active cross-border operations or expanding internationally
- Produce complex formatted documents (PDFs with charts, tables, layouts)
- Industries: legal, financial services, manufacturing, pharma, technology, consulting

### Signal types to prioritize
- Cross-border M&A (high document volume, time pressure)
- Regulatory compliance requiring multilingual docs (hard deadlines)
- International expansion into non-English markets

### Contact hierarchy
1. Localization Manager / Translation Lead
2. Legal Operations
3. International Operations
4. Content / Marketing Operations
5. COO / VP Operations (at smaller companies)

### Disqualifiers
- Under 20 employees
- Purely domestic, no international signals
- Already has dedicated localization team with TMS (SDL Trados, memoQ, Smartling)
- Government/military (long procurement cycles)
